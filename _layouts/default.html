Vidhi Jain

I work on improving robots for assistance in everyday household tasks. I am a PhD student at the Robotics Institute(RI), part of the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). I am advised by Yonatan Bisk.

Before this, I worked at Meta AI (formerly FAIR), as an AI resident. I primarily worked on the Robovarchi project with Akshara Rai and Yixin Lin. I also worked as a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, advised by Amit Deshpande and Navin Goyal.

I received a Masters in Robotics from CMU RI working on on interpretable embodied AI with Katia Sycara. I earned a Bachelors in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, where I worked in Rahul Banerjee's lab. I collaborated with Aaron Courville at Mila, Quebec on deep generative models.
